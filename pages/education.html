A World Where no Child's life is Uneducated

Mission Education works with the objective of empowering underprivileged children by providing education and wellness support. The programme is well design for the New Education Policy (2020) and promote lifelong learning opportunities for all. Mission Education works with children (3-18 years) living in difficult circumstances, children from poor families, remote villages and hard to reach areas.
